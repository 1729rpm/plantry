Rice tossed with cubes of paneer, peppers and spring onion.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Paneer | 120 | g |
| Capsicum | 60 | g |
| Spring Onion | 30 | g |
| Carrot | 50 | g |

## Recipe

1. Use cooled cooked rice and lightly pan fry the paneer cubes.
2. Stir fry diced carrot, capsicum and spring onion whites on high heat.
3. Add the rice with soy sauce, salt and pepper and toss.
4. Fold in the paneer and spring onion greens.
