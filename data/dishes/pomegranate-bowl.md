Fresh pomegranate arils to eat alongside breakfast, crisp and jewel-like.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Pomegranate | 150 | g |

## Recipe

1. Halve the pomegranate and tap out the arils into a bowl.
2. Serve fresh alongside the breakfast main.
