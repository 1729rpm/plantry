Diced cucumber and tomato tossed with onion, lemon and coriander, a cooling lunch side.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Cucumber | 150 | g |
| Tomato | 150 | g |
| Onion | 50 | g |
| Lemon | 1 | pcs |
| Coriander Leaf | 10 | g |
| Green Chilli | 1 | pcs |

## Recipe

1. Dice the cucumber, tomato and onion fine and slit the green chilli.
2. Toss with lemon juice and salt.
3. Fold in the coriander and serve fresh.
