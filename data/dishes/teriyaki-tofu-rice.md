Crisp tofu in a sweet soy teriyaki glaze served over rice with greens.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Tofu | 250 | g |
| Broccoli | 100 | g |
| Soy Sauce | 30 | ml |
| Ginger | 8 | g |
| Garlic | 8 | g |
| Spring Onion | 20 | g |

## Recipe

1. Press and cube the tofu and sear till golden and crisp.
2. Simmer soy sauce, ginger, garlic and a little sugar into a glossy teriyaki.
3. Toss the tofu in the glaze till coated.
4. Steam the broccoli till just tender.
5. Serve the tofu and broccoli over rice, scattered with spring onion.
