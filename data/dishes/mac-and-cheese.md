Macaroni in a creamy cheese sauce, a comforting bowl.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Pasta | 120 | g |
| Cheese | 60 | g |
| Milk | 150 | ml |

## Recipe

1. Boil the macaroni in salted water till just done and drain.
2. Make a roux with butter and flour, then whisk in the milk to a smooth sauce.
3. Melt in most of the grated cheese with salt, pepper and a little mustard.
4. Fold in the pasta, top with the rest of the cheese and serve hot.
