Cubed ripe mango to eat alongside breakfast, sweet and juicy.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Mango | 200 | g |

## Recipe

1. Peel the mango and cut the flesh off the stone into cubes.
2. Serve fresh in a bowl alongside the breakfast main.
