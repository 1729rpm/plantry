Savoury gram flour pancakes with onion, tomato and coriander.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Onion | 50 | g |
| Tomato | 50 | g |
| Green Chilli | 2 | pcs |
| Coriander Leaf | 10 | g |

## Recipe

1. Whisk besan with water, salt and turmeric into a smooth pouring batter.
2. Stir in finely chopped onion, tomato, green chilli and coriander.
3. Ladle onto a hot greased tawa and spread into a thin round.
4. Cook both sides on medium heat till set and lightly browned.
