## Recipe

1. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 15 minutes, then drain.
2. Bring the rice to a boil with measured water and a little salt.
3. Cover and cook on low until the water is absorbed.
4. Rest off the heat for five minutes, then fluff with a fork.
