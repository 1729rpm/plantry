Crisp fried cauliflower tossed in a spicy garlic Indo Chinese sauce.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Cauliflower | 200 | g |
| Cornflour | 30 | g |
| Capsicum | 50 | g |
| Spring Onion | 30 | g |
| Garlic | 10 | g |

## Recipe

1. Coat the cauliflower florets in a cornflour and flour batter and fry till crisp.
2. Stir fry garlic, capsicum and spring onion whites on high heat.
3. Add soy sauce, chilli sauce and a splash of water and bring to a glaze.
4. Toss in the fried cauliflower, coat quickly and top with spring onion greens.
