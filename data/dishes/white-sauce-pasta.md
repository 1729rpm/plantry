Pasta and vegetables in a creamy white sauce.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Pasta | 120 | g |
| Milk | 150 | ml |
| Cheese | 40 | g |
| Capsicum | 40 | g |
| Sweet Corn | 40 | g |

## Recipe

1. Boil the pasta till just done and saute the diced capsicum and corn.
2. Make a roux with butter and flour and whisk in the milk to a smooth sauce.
3. Season with salt, pepper, herbs and the grated cheese.
4. Toss the pasta and vegetables through the sauce and serve hot.
