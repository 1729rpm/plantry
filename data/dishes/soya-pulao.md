One pot rice with soya chunks and vegetables, a high protein meal.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Soyabean Chunk | 80 | g |
| Onion | 80 | g |
| Carrot | 50 | g |
| Green Pea | 50 | g |

## Recipe

1. Soak the soya chunks in hot salted water and squeeze dry.
2. Fry whole spices and onion, add ginger garlic and the vegetables.
3. Add the soya chunks and soaked rice and toss with salt.
4. Add measured water and cook covered till done, then rest and fluff.
