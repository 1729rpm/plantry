Peeled fresh litchis to eat alongside breakfast, light and fragrant.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Litchi | 150 | g |

## Recipe

1. Peel the litchis and remove the seeds.
2. Serve fresh in a bowl alongside the breakfast main.
