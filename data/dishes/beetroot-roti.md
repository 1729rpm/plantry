Soft wheat flatbreads kneaded with grated beetroot, earthy and a deep pink.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Beetroot | 100 | g |

## Recipe

1. Grate the beetroot fine and knead it into wheat flour with salt, a little oil and water to a soft dough, then rest 15 minutes.
2. Divide and roll each ball into a round, dusting with flour.
3. Cook on a hot tawa, flipping once bubbles appear.
4. Puff on a low flame and brush with ghee.
