Karnataka one pot rice and lentils with vegetables and a special spice blend.

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Quantity | Unit |
|------------|----------|------|
| Toor Dal | 60 | g |
| Carrot | 60 | g |
| French Bean | 60 | g |
| Green Pea | 40 | g |
| Tomato | 50 | g |

## Recipe

1. Cook the rice and toor dal together till soft.
2. Boil the chopped vegetables with tamarind water till tender.
3. Combine rice, dal, vegetables and bisi bele bath masala with water to a thick consistency.
4. Simmer together and finish with a ghee tempering of cashew and curry leaves.
